### Key Strengths
- **Exceptional walkability** — full score of 10/10, driven by dense pedestrian infrastructure, mixed-use zoning, and extensive sidewalk network on NW 23rd Avenue corridor
- **Outstanding healthcare access** — Walgreens pharmacy at just 38m distance, plus 5 other healthcare facilities within 1km (Integrative Primary Care Associates, James P. Thomas MD, Fred Meyer Pharmacy, Portland Dermatology Clinic)
- **Excellent transit access** — Portland Streetcar stops (NW 23rd & Marshall at 129m, NW Northrup & 22nd at 174m, NW Lovejoy & 22nd at 256m) plus TriMet bus lines all within 400m
- **Vibrant shopping corridor** — 16 independent retail shops on NW 23rd Ave (Title Nine, Faherty, Marine Layer, Cotopaxi, Arc'teryx, prAna)
- **Rich cafe culture** — 14 cafes including Portal Tea (210m), Case Study Coffee Roasters (140m), Kirari West (200m), Memento Mori Cafe (260m)
- **Good fitness options** — 11 sports/fitness venues including YogaSix (260m), Orangetheory Fitness (318m), Portland Aikikai
- **Rich public art scene** — 8 documented artworks including sculptures and murals within 1km (Large Metal Pigs at 240m, Howard's Way sculpture at 920m)
- **Proximity to diverse dining** — Thai, Burmese, Indian, Italian, Vietnamese cuisines nearby on NW 23rd Ave

### Areas for Improvement
- **Education** — Only 2 schools within 1km (Bridges Middle School, Class Academy); elementary school options remain limited
- **Parks** — Only 3 small playgrounds within 1km; no full-size parks or green spaces. The nearest parks are in the Pearl District area
- **Entertainment** — No cinemas, theaters, or music venues within 1km. Portland's entertainment district is concentrated downtown (~3km south)
- **Healthcare services** — No general practitioner services nearby; only pharmacies and specialists
- **Groceries** — Only 3 options within 1.5km; Zupan's (955m) is the closest full supermarket
